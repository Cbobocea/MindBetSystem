Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2025

At Mindbet Systems, we value transparency and privacy. Our goal is to offer smart, fair, and secure football match simulations and betting analysis tools while respecting your data.

Information We Collect

  • Email address and encrypted password (for account creation and login)
  • Simulation counts per day (to manage free-tier access)
  • Subscription status via Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • Contact form submissions (via SendGrid)
  • Optional usage analytics (anonymized)

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate and authorize access to simulation features
  • To determine if you've reached the daily simulation quota
  • To notify you of subscription status or new features (if opted in)
  • To prevent fraud and misuse of the platform

Third-Party Services

We securely integrate with the following platforms:

  • Stripe – For handling payments and subscriptions
  • SendGrid – For managing emails sent through the contact form
  • Google reCAPTCHA – To protect against bots

We do not share or sell your personal data to any other third parties.

Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Request deletion of your account
  • Update or change your email or password
  • Request export of your account-related data

Email us at support@mindbet.ai to make any requests.

Data Security

We use SSL encryption and modern security best practices to protect your information. Passwords are hashed before storing, and we never save payment credentials directly.

Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this policy for clarity or compliance. Any major changes will be announced on our platform. We encourage users to review this page periodically.
