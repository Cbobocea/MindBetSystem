About Our eSports Predictions

Our eSports prediction engine uses advanced algorithms and statistical models to simulate matches across multiple games including CS:GO, League of Legends, Dota 2, and Valorant.

The simulations take into account team rankings, player performance, head-to-head history, and game-specific metrics to deliver accurate predictions.
